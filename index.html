Welcome

I am an associate professor at Arizona State University. My research focuses on strategies to improve supply chain resilience , social responsibilities, and to mitigate supply chain risk propagation.

If you have any questions or suggestions related to my research, please reach out at yimin_wang@asu.edu. I am always interested in hearing your suggestions and comments.
